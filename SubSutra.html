13 Sub-Sutras
S.No. Name Meaning Uses
Sub-Sutra 1 Anurupyena Proportionately This sub-Sutra aids in quickly determining the last digit of a product.
Sub-Sutra 2 Adyamdyenantya-mantye-na First by first and last by last Useful for solving problems where the last two digits are required.
Sub-Sutra 3 Sisyate Sesasamjnah Remainder remains constant This sub-Sutra extends the “Ekadhikena Purvena” technique for squaring numbers closer to the base
Sub-Sutra 4 Vestanam By Osculation Helps in solving linear equations and balance problems
Sub-Sutra 5 Kevalaih Saptakam Gunyat For 7 the Multiplicand is 143 Offers additional methods for solving ratio and proportion problems.
Sub-Sutra 6 Samuccayagunitah The Sum of the coefficients in the product Useful for solving problems involving the product of two sums.
Sub-Sutra 7 Lopanasthapanabhyam By Alternate Elimination and Retention Aids in simplifying algebraic expressions.
Sub-Sutra 8 Yavadunam Tavadunam Lessen by the Deficiency Provides an alternative approach for finding squares.
Sub-Sutra 9 Yavadunam Tavadunam Varganca Yojayet Whatever the Deficiency lessen by that amount and set up the Square of the Deficiency Useful for quick calculations involving the last digit of numbers
Sub-Sutra 10 Antyayordasake Last Totalling 10 Enables efficient calculations when focusing on the last two digits.
Sub-Sutra 11 Antyayoreva Only the Last Terms A technique for adding fractions with common denominators
Sub-Sutra 12 Vilokanam By Observation Facilitates quick division.
Sub-Sutra 13 Gunitasmuccayah Samuccayagunitah The Product of the Sum is the Sum of the Products A technique for division, especially when dealing with recurring decimals.
