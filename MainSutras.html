16 Sutras
S.No. Name Meaning Uses
Sutra 1 Ekadhikina Purvena By one more than the previous one This Sutra simplifies squaring numbers close to base values
Sutra 2 Nikhilam Navatashcaramam Dashatah All from 9 and the last from 10 A powerful technique for subtraction, especially useful when dealing with numbers close to multiples of 10.
Sutra 3 Urdhva-Tiryagbyham Vertically and crosswise This Sutra streamlines multiplication, especially useful for multiplying large numbers.
Sutra 4 Paraavartya Yojayet Transpose and adjust This technique aids in simplifying complex mathematical problems involving equations and variables.
Sutra 5 Shunyam Saamyasamuccaye When the sum is the same that sum is zero An effective approach for solving algebraic equations with equal sums on both sides.
Sutra 6 Anurupyena- Sunyamanyat If one is in ratio, the other is zero This Sutra is indispensable for solving proportionality problems.
Sutra 7 Sopaantyadvayamantyam The ultimate and twice the penultimate Simplifies division and finding square roots.
Sutra 8 Ekanyunena Purvena By one less than the previous one A technique that encourages quick, intuitive solutions based on patterns and observations.
Sutra 9 Sankalana-Vyavakalanabhyam By addition and by subtraction This Sutra offers techniques for both addition and subtraction, enabling quick calculations
Sutra 10 Puranapuranabyham By the completion or Non-completion This Sutra aids in finding fractions and complements, simplifying various mathematical operations.
Sutra 11 Chalana-Kalanabyham Differences and Similarities Useful for problems involving ratios and proportions
Sutra 12 Yaavadunam Whatever the extent of its deficiency This Sutra facilitates the multiplication of large numbers by breaking them down into smaller, more manageable parts
Sutra 13 Gunitasamuchyah The product of the sum is equal to the sum of the product This Sutra helps in solving problems where a specific value is derived from a general one
Sutra 14 Vyashtisamanstih Part and Whole This Sutra is handy for finding individual components from a group
Sutra 15 Shesanyankena Charamena The remainders by the last digit Useful for quick summations, especially when dealing with a series of numbers
